Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions portal. The application process includes submitting academic records, test scores, a personal statement, and letters of recommendation. Our admissions team will guide you through each step.

What are the application deadlines?

Application deadlines vary by program. Generally, fall semester applications are due by June 15th, and spring semester applications by November 15th. We recommend applying early for better consideration.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options including scholarships, grants, work-study programs, and student loans. Over 70% of our students receive some form of financial assistance.

Can international students apply?

Absolutely! We welcome international students from around the world. We provide dedicated support for visa processing, accommodation, and cultural integration.
